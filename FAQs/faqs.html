Frequently Asked Questions

What are my loans for?

Watu Credit has granted you a loan to enable you to purchase the Vehicle.

what documents do I need to sign to access the loan?

You need to sign a loan agreement which details the terms of the loan and a security agreement which provides that the Vehicle is security for the loan and which allows Watu to sell the Vehicle if there is a default under the loan agreement.

What are the requirements for one to get financing from PataBoda?

To qualify for a loan, one is required to have the following:

  • Original National ID (borrower and one guarantor)
  • KRA pin certificate
  • Borrower's age, 18-19 yrs(must be accompanied by guardian) 26-65 as normal
  • Two referees above 18 years old
  • A minimum down payment of Kshs 20,000 for motorbike financing and from Kshs 50,000 for tuk-tuk financing
  • A Safaricom phone number that is registered for M-Pesa and has been in use for the last 6 months.
